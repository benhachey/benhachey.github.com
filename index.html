Ben Hachey - About

I am a data science professional of 15+ years. I build and lead teams that develop machine-in-the-loop tools to amplify human intelligence. My key expertise is in product-driven AI, interactive ML and NLP.

At Red Marker, we give domain experts the power to develop and manage executable compliance rules. At Hugo.ai, we built a session search tool to find and summarise people's online profiles in five minutes.

I supervise PhD/Honours students at The University of Sydney, where I was previously an Early Career Research Fellow, and developed and taught the Graduate Certificate and Master of Data Science.
