The Edinburgh Regularised RE Corpora consist of publicly available
data sets (ACE 2004, ACE 2005 and BioInfer) that have been
standardised for evaluation of multi-type RE across domains.


FURTHER INFORMATION:

* An overview can be found on the corpus home page:
  http://benhachey.info/data/gre/

* A detailed description can be found in the corpus paper:
  Ben Hachey, Claire Grover and Richard Tobin (2011). Datasets for
  Generic Relation Extraction. Journal of Natural Language
  Engineering.
  http://dx.doi.org/10.1017/S1351324911000106
  http://benhachey.info/pubs/hachey-nle11-datasets-preprint.pdf

* The format is described in the corpus DTD:
  http://web.science.mq.edu.au/~bhachey/data/gre/reXML.dtd
